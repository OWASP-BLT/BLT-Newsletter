# Welcome to the BLT Newsletter!

Thank you for subscribing to the OWASP Bug Logging Tool (BLT) newsletter. We're excited to have you as part of our community!

## What to expect

Every month, we'll send you:
- **Platform Stats**: See how the BLT ecosystem is growing.
- **New Features**: Be the first to know about our latest tools and improvements.
- **Security Insights**: Tips and best practices for secure coding and bug hunting.
- **Community Highlights**: Recognizing our top contributors and success stories.

## Get Involved

BLT is an open-source project, and we'd love for you to contribute:
- [Check out our GitHub](https://github.com/OWASP-BLT)
- [Read the Documentation](https://owasp-blt.github.io/documentation/)

If you have any questions, just reply to this email or open an issue on our tracker.

Happy Bug Hunting!
The OWASP BLT Team
